Post Office Supervisor Information

Post Office staff handle all parcels that arrive through government or courier postal services. Their role in the system is to log in, scan parcel barcodes, and register each parcel in the BV ParcelPing system. When a parcel is scanned, its details are sent to the central database and a notification is triggered to the respective student. Post Office staff also update parcel statuses such as “Received,” “Out for Delivery,” or “Transferred to Hostel.” Their actions ensure that all postal parcels are digitally tracked and students receive timely updates.
