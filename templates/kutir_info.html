Kutir Supervisor Information

Kutir staff manage parcels that arrive through online shopping platforms such as Amazon, Flipkart, and other e-commerce services. Their responsibility is to log in to the system, scan the parcel barcode when it arrives, and update its status in the system. Once scanned, the student automatically receives a notification. Kutir staff also update when parcels are ready for delivery or have been dispatched to hostels. This makes sure that online shopping parcels are properly recorded, tracked, and delivered without confusion or loss.
