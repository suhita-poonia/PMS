Frequently Asked Questions

What is BV ParcelPing?
BV ParcelPing is a digital parcel management system designed for Banasthali Vidyapith to help students track parcels and receive instant notifications.
Who can use BV ParcelPing?
The system can be used by students, Post Office staff, Kutir staff, delivery staff, and system administrators.
How will I know when my parcel arrives?
Once your parcel is scanned at the Post Office or Kutir, you will receive an instant notification with its status and location.
Can I track my parcel status online?
Yes, students can log in and track parcel statuses such as Received, Out for Delivery, and Delivered to Hostel.
What types of parcels are handled?
BV ParcelPing handles both postal parcels from the Post Office and online shopping parcels received at Kutir.
Is my personal information secure?
Yes, the system uses secure authentication and role-based access to ensure student and staff data privacy.
Who should I contact if there is an issue with my parcel?
In case of any issue, students should contact the Kutir or Post Office helpdesk for assistance.
